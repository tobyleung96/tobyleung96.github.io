Revit: Office Construction

Introduction
In today's world, Revit has quickly climbed to be the ultimate architectural tool. There is simply no other tool that can produce all the construction documents necessary with such swift integration. As a project to learn the tool, this office design explores the multitude of possibilities that Revit supports (e.g. floorplans, RCPs, sections, schedules, millwork, renders etc.).
