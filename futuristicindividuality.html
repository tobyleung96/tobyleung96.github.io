Futuristic Individuality

In my perspective, design should be exciting and celebrated. Each project should have an unique identity and characteristics that makes for a memorable interaction with the user. In order to rid myself of banalness from repeating history, I aim to create beautiful pieces that still have places in the future. When designing, one must consider the final outcome’s lifespan. Through my Information Science minor, I’ve been given a unique technological perspective that allows me to create in the 21st century. The influence of the digital age is sweeping over design like a storm, and I aim to keep up with it by building with the future in mind.
